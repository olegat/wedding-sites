Direct flights into Zaragoza (ZAZ) are limited. There’s one round-trip daily from London Stansted (STN) and one from Charleroi (CRL), both with Ryanair. Flights don’t operate every day of the week.

Many more flights are available to Madrid, Barcelona, or Bilbao. These cities are around 3 hours’ drive from Zaragoza. Madrid and Barcelona have high-speed AVE trains to Zaragoza (about 1.5 hours).

Tourism & Pilares

While you’re in Zaragoza, visit the stunning Basilica del Pilar, the Aljafería Palace, and stroll through the charming historic city center. During October, the city celebrates “Fiestas del Pilar,” with parades, concerts, food stalls, and cultural events. The festival dates back centuries, honoring the Virgin of the Pillar, patroness of the city.
